Cookies Recipe

Description

This is a simple recipe for chocolate chip cookies. It only requires a few ingredients and around 30 minutes to make! The serving size for this recipe is 12 cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon of baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 2-3 cups of chocolate chips (your choice)
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 2 cups of brown sugar

Steps

  1. Combine the flour and baking soda in a large bowl
  2. Melt the butter
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the melted butter, vanilla extract, egg, and sugar; mix well
  4. Add in the dry ingredients and mix all combonents together until well combined
  5. Pour in the chocolate chips and mix thoroughly
  6. Bake cookies in the oven for 10 minutes at 350 degrees Farenheit
